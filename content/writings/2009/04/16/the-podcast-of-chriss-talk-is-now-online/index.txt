I have attended the [Yahoo! University Talk in NCTU](http://registrano.com/events/090416ydn) today. [Chris](http://www.wait-till-i.com/) is a very good speaker. He provides a very clear overview, and make people laugh from start to finish. You can [check out his slide](http://www.wait-till-i.com/2009/04/16/the-road-to-professional-web-development-my-university-talk-in-taiwan/).

I have recorded the talk, but missed the first 5 mins or so. I have already asked Chris for the permission, so you can [download the MP3](http://dl.getdropbox.com/u/132028/Christian%20Heilmann%20%28NCTU%29.mp3) from my Dropbox.

Chris will be in [OSDC](http://osdc.tw/) too, and I will look forward to get more insight from Chris, like the YUI3 or YQL development.

我今天參加[Yahoo!奇摩交大校園講座](http://registrano.com/events/090416ydn)。[Chris](http://www.wait-till-i.com/)實在是個厲害的講者。他今天主要的內容是網頁標準開發的歷史進程概述，非常的清楚明白，而且讓大家從頭到尾笑聲不斷。你可以[在他的網站看到這份投影片](http://www.wait-till-i.com/2009/04/16/the-road-to-professional-web-development-my-university-talk-in-taiwan/)。

我今天有用新買的 WireTap Studio 錄音，不過前面五分鐘左右忘記錄到了。我有寄信問 Chris，他同意我把這份錄音檔放在網路上。有興趣可以從我的 Dropbox [下載 MP3 檔](http://dl.getdropbox.com/u/132028/Christian%20Heilmann%20%28NCTU%29.mp3)。

Chris 也會去 [OSDC](http://osdc.tw/)，希望能聽到更深入的內容，像是還沒用過的 YUI3 或是 YQL。
